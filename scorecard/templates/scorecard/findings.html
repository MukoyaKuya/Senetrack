NOMINATED SENATORS 2023–2026 Term

Nominated Senators Score Lower on Average — Structural Disadvantage

{{ nominated_gap|default:"–" }}

pts below elected avg

Nominated senators automatically score 0 on the County Representation pillar (5 pts) since they do not represent a specific county. When comparing within the full senate, this structural disadvantage is compounded if nominated senators are also newer entrants with less Hansard coverage. SENETRACK recommends comparing nominated senators only within their own cohort.

See the Methodology page for the compensating bills bonus applied to nominated senators.
